Elementary Books

What are elementary books?

Our first project “Elementary Books” is a concept of sixty unique and individually made different NFT books. Each of these books have unique features and many more which will help to establish school libraries, book donation campaigns and from the purchase of each book of WNF WNF will support education the expenses of sixty students. This project will also help such students and families who are economically low. Some of our NFTs are Rarer than others.

Owning one of our NFTs will change a random stranger's life forever, donate a library to a school and Give a access to the World NFT fund's campaigns.

This initiative will change many lives. Our goal is to help these people have a better life and quality living.

In this explanation process, we would like to answer the 5W (, what, where, why, when, who1h(how) from this project.

What is THe World NFT Fund?On what does it focus?

The world NFT fund is an organization to help those indeed in this modern world. While the world is growing more and more into new virtual discoveries like The Metaverse and NFTs. We focus on using these modern arts and technologies to help needy people. The basic concept of us is to sell digital NFTs and replace them with the same physical assets in need (for example: if we mint two tables and two chairs of NFT and when someone makes a purchase. The purchased product gets replaced with the physical ones for the kids to learn at our organization or home). Our main objective is to provide poor, needy, and capable kids with a more modern way of receiving knowledge and helping their families by providing them the best that we can get for them.

Where does the idea evolve from?

The idea of the social NFT work starts from a small room containing some students from poor and needy families to help them.

Why?

Illiteracy is the root cause of all social crimes that have broken off people from getting the quality of life and modernization. The primary milestone of us is to reach out to many people as we can with the help of NFTs and make this world a better place for them.

When?

The present is the right time to start everything that we can because the world is initially growing towards a digital and virtual palace it is the right time to take a good purpose from NFTs and digital arts.

How?

We plan to help people with digital art pieces of our own from which we replace it with a physical version of it. The royalties obtained from NFTs will be used for donations and other social activities. We tend to reach those millions of NFT philanthropists, investors, buyers who come and join us at this revolutionary movement that we're working in. Your one simple purchase can make someone's future better.
